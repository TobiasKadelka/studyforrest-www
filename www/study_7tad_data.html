Inter-subject response similarities
Distributed voxel time serie correlation beyond TVA

7-Tesla fMRI dataset from complex natural stimulation with an audio movie

BOLD time series correlation on anatomically aligned data reveal consistent brain responses to quasi natural auditory stimulation across all participants. Areas of maximum similarity in both hemispheres are centered on the anterolateral portion of the Planum Temporale — part of the auditory ventrolateral pathway and known to be involved in processing complex sounds and speech.

The figure below shows the relation of the areas of maximum similarity with the localization of the human temporal voice area (TVA, Pernet et al., submitted; see NeuroVault for more information).
