Physiological measures and confounds
Cardiac and respiratory trace, and motion estimates

Cardiac and respiratory trace

These data have been obtain simultaneously with the fMRI recording during stimulation. Files are in plain text format (compressed) for each scanning run separately and includes the MR scanner's volume acquisition trigger signal for precise temporal alignment. These data are useful for de-noising of fMRI time series. The interactive plot below shows an exemplary minute of data at 50 Hz sampling rate (actual data is sampled at 200 Hz).

Usage: Click on legend items to disable/enable individual data types. Click and drag on the lower focus chart to zoom in on a time segment. Drag a zoom window to scroll through time.

Participant motion estimates

These data have been estimated from the raw BOLD images prior to distortion correction. Files are provided in a six-column plain text format for each fMRI scanning run separately.
