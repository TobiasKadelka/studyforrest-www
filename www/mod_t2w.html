T2-weighted MRI scans
Images with fluid-tissue contrast

In T2-weighted MR images stationary water (like cerebrospinal fluid) appears white, while brain tissue is darker. Blood vessels are black as the magnetized protons have been flushed away by the time of the measurement. More information on this image type.

Technical details

Full-brain volumetric images (384x384x274) in NIfTI format recorded with a Philips MR scanner at 3 Tesla using a 3D TSE sequence (0.67 mm isotropic voxel size). Facial features have been removed from these images for de-identification.
